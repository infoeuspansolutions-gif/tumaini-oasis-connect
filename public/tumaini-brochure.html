About Us

Tumaini Gardens is a serene, nature-inspired lodge and event sanctuary located along the Nairobi-Namanga Highway in Kajiado County, immediately after Merishaw School. Approximately 67 km (1.5 hours) from Nairobi, we are a popular destination for getaways, team-building, weddings, and conferences.

Getting Here

  • From Nairobi: Take Mombasa Road → turn onto Nairobi-Namanga Highway → drive ~67 km → look for Merishaw School → Tumaini Gardens is immediately after.
  • Distance: ~60–67 km · Drive Time: ~1.5 hours
  • GPS: Search "Tumaini Gardens Isinya" on Google Maps
